Walk Spot
UX Design, Visual Design

It's challenging at times for many loved ones to remember to take their medication. With this iOS app, family and friends can now monitor prescriptions with their loved ones. Love ones will be reassured that maintaining their health is a team effort, and no loved one needs to do it alone.

User Story
It’s 2:55pm at the office, and Jessica is finishing up work.
Jessica wants to attend her yoga class at 5:00pm.
However, Jessica feels obligated to walk her dog, Rover, at that time.
It’s 3:05pm, and Jessica logs into the Walk Spot app.
She schedules a walk for Rover in order to make her yoga class.
She schedules Rover to be picked up for his walk today at 4:30pm.
Jessica is now relieved that she can make her yoga class on time and Rover will have his walk.
She makes it on time for a 5pm yoga class, while Rover gets to have his walk around the neighborhood.
Both Jessica and Rover are both happy campers when they come back from their activities.
